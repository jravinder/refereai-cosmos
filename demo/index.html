Your Game, Your Rules

Five Sports. One AI.

Whether it's gully cricket in Mumbai, park tennis in London, or pickleball in your backyard — RefereAI understands the physics of your game.

Cricket

2.5 billion fans worldwide. Massive gully cricket culture.

What Cosmos Detects

  • Ball trajectory from bowler to batsman
  • Boundary 4s and 6s (ball crossing rope)
  • Wickets and stump hits
  • Wide and no-ball calls
  • Shot classification (drive, pull, cut, sweep)
  • Swing, seam, and spin analysis

Tennis

1 billion fans. Park and club players everywhere.

What Cosmos Detects

  • Ball position relative to court lines (in/out)
  • Shot type: forehand, backhand, serve, volley
  • Serve placement (wide, T, body)
  • Spin analysis (topspin, slice, flat)
  • Aces and double faults
  • Rally dynamics and court positioning

Pickleball

Fastest growing sport in the US. Explosive community.

What Cosmos Detects

  • Kitchen (non-volley zone) violations
  • Dink shots vs drives vs lobs
  • Ball trajectory relative to boundaries
  • Serve legality (underhand, below waist)
  • Double-bounce rule compliance
  • Player positioning relative to kitchen line

Badminton

Huge in Asia. Fast-paced rallies and smash speeds over 300 km/h.

What Cosmos Detects

  • Shuttlecock trajectory and deceleration
  • Smash vs clear vs drop shot classification
  • Court boundary detection (in/out)
  • Serve rule compliance
  • Net violations
  • Rally patterns and footwork analysis

Table Tennis

Popular worldwide. Spin analysis is everything.

What Cosmos Detects

  • Ball spin (topspin, backspin, sidespin) from paddle angle
  • Ball trajectory relative to table edges
  • Serve legality (open palm, toss height)
  • Let detection (net clips)
  • Edge balls vs clean hits
  • Rally speed and placement patterns
