## Scene 1: THE SPARK (0:00–0:12)
**Camera:** Wide, golden hour, warm tones
**Shot:** Young professional lounging on modern minimalist couch. Phone buzzes — RefereAI notification. Curiosity sparked.
**Mood:** Relaxed, casual. Manus/OpenAI launch video aesthetic.

## Scene 2: THE CHALLENGE (0:12–0:25)
**Camera:** Medium shot, natural office lighting
**Shot:** Office break room. Colleague says "Think you can beat my line on this? Quick game?" Protagonist pulls out phone, taps RefereAI. No setup needed.
**Dialogue:** "You're on."

## Scene 3: CRICKET IN THE PARK (0:25–0:50)
**Camera:** Dynamic — wide establishing + close-ups
**Shot:** Friends playing cricket in a park (Mumbai/Sydney vibes). Phone propped on bag. Live score overlay: "32/2 (4.3 overs)". Cosmos AI call-out: "LBW Analysis: ball pitched in line, impact on middle stump — OUT"
**Key moment:** Friends react to the AI making a call they were arguing about.

## Scene 4: TENNIS ON PUBLIC COURT (0:50–1:10)
**Camera:** Aerial drone establishing → close-ups
**Shot:** Two players on a public clay court. Phone on fence. Score updating real-time. Close-up of phone showing serve speed: 110 MPH. Live tracking overlay.
**Director note:** Drone shot is the visual hook. Digital overlay must feel integrated, not pasted on.

## Scene 5: GLOBAL MONTAGE (1:10–1:35)
**Camera:** Fast cuts, diverse locations
**Shot:** Quick cuts across 5 sports/locations:
1. Pickleball — blue outdoor court, American suburbs
2. Badminton — indoor gym, Southeast Asia
3. Table tennis — office break room
4. Tennis — clay court, Europe
5. Cricket — park, India

Each shows: phone propped → camera watching → live score → friends reacting
**Text overlay:** "One AI. Five Sports. Every Court."

## Scene 6: GETTING SERIOUS (1:35–1:55)
**Camera:** Cinematic slow-motion (120fps), dramatic backlighting at dusk
**Shot:** Solo tennis player training intensely. Powerful serve. RefereAI tracking: serve speed, technique analysis, spin rate. Skeletal tracking visualization.
**Text overlay:** "KNOW YOUR GAME"
**Mood shift:** Fun → professional. This is where the product becomes serious.

**Camera:** Screen recording + motion graphics
**Shot:** Clean architecture diagram animated left-to-right:
- Input Source (4K Camera Feed)

**Show:** Actual app screenshots, Cosmos reasoning output.
**Purpose:** Credibility moment for judges — this is real tech, not vaporware.
